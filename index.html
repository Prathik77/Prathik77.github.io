Privacy Policy for Tamil AI

Last Updated: September 6, 2024

Quick Summary: Tamil AI processes your voice and camera data in real-time to provide Tamil language learning features. We don't store your personal data permanently.

Introduction

Tamil AI is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Tamil AI mobile application.

Information We Collect

1. Audio Data

  • Speech Recognition: When you use the speech-to-text feature, we process your voice recordings to convert Tamil speech to text
  • Processing: Audio data is sent to Google Cloud Speech-to-Text API for processing
  • Storage: Audio data is not permanently stored on our servers

2. Image Data

  • Camera Access: When you use the handwriting recognition feature, we access your device's camera
  • OCR Processing: Images are sent to Google Cloud Vision API for Tamil text recognition
  • Storage: Images are not permanently stored on our servers

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide Tamil language learning features
  • Convert speech to text using AI services
  • Recognize Tamil handwriting from images
  • Improve app functionality and user experience

Third-Party Services

Our app uses Google Cloud services:

  • Google Cloud Speech-to-Text API: For Tamil speech recognition
  • Google Cloud Vision API: For Tamil handwriting recognition

These services are subject to Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Data Security

  • All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS
  • Audio and image data are processed securely through Google Cloud services
  • We do not store your personal audio or image data
  • All processing is done in real-time and data is not retained

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of data collection
  • Contact us with privacy concerns

Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Data Retention

  • Audio recordings: Not stored, processed in real-time only
  • Images: Not stored, processed in real-time only
  • Usage analytics: Retained for up to 2 years for app improvement

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@tamilai.app

Compliance

This Privacy Policy complies with Apple App Store Guidelines, Google Play Store Requirements, GDPR, and CCPA.

By using Tamil AI, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.
